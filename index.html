Tianlu Wang

I am a research scientist at Facebook AI Research. I did my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Virginia, where I was advised by Prof. Vicente Ordóñez Román. Before that, I received my bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Zhejiang University, China. I have a broad interest in exploring topics concerning fairness, robustness and accountability in machine learning models, especially in computer vision and natural language processing systems.
